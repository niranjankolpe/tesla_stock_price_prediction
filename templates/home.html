Welcome to the Tesla Stock Price Predictor

Predicting stock prices is an exciting yet challenging task...

Project Overview:

Our goal is to develop a predictive model that utilizes machine learning algorithms to forecast Tesla's stock prices.

Key Features:

  • Real-time Data: We fetch and analyze up-to-date stock data from reliable sources.
  • Machine Learning Models: Our project employs state-of-the-art machine learning techniques for accurate predictions.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easily interact with the project and obtain predictions through a simple and intuitive interface.

How It Works

Input the following data to the Predictor:
  1. Date : The date of prediction
  2. Open : The opening price of the day
  3. High : The highest price of day
  4. Low : The lowest price of the day
  5. Close : The closed price of the day
  6. Volume : The amount of stocks traded price of the day
Output generated is nothing but the prediction value:

Adj Closed : Adjusted Close is the stock's closing price that has been amended to include any distributions/corporate actions that occurs before next days open.
